An Evening with Gaylord Schanilec

Please join us on October 21, 2024 at the Logan Library at 7:00pm. There is a presentation by Gaylord Schanilec, an artist and co-author with Terry Tempest Williams - "Oracle Bones" (Red Butte Press, 2024). The presentation is free and open to the public.

Sponsored by: Diane and Bill Oblock, Utah State University: Merrill-Cazier Library Special Collections and Archive, College of Humanitics and Social Sciences, Department of English, Caine College of the Arts, Department of Art and Design, Quinney College of Natural Resources, Wildlife Resources Department, Nora Eccles Harrison Museum of Art, James Kennedy, (Emeritus Professor), Terry and David Peak, Frank and Deborah Ascione, Janet Osborne, SpringHill Suites by Marriott Logan
