Benefits of Joining

In addition to the joy of supporting an important growing community center and hub of activity, being a Friend of the Logan Library will give you these benefits:

  • Opportunities to lend your talents in support of the Library and community;
  • Contributing to improving the quality of life in our town;
  • A chance to meet other community or library-minded people;
  • Email updates;
  • Invitation to special events.
