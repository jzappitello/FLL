About Us

Friends of the
Logan Library

Friends of the Logan Library was formed in 2013-2014, born out of a shared passion for fostering literacy and community engagement.

One of our more regular activities has been our recurring book sales, events that not only provide book lovers with treasures to discover but also serve as vital fundraisers for our cause. Through the collective efforts of our dedicated volunteers and generous supporters, we've been able to contribute significantly to the enhancement of library facilities and resources.

In the coming years, our impact will continue to extend far beyond financial contributions. We will stand steadfastly beside the library and its dedicated staff, offering essential support in various forms. Together, we will shape not just the future of our library, but also enrich the lives of those who call Logan home.
